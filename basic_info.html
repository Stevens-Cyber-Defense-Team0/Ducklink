About SCDT
Introduction
Hello there, and welcome to SCDT! We quite literally steal flags and break things. Our club participates in monthly/bi-monthly CTFs (although we're trying to see if we could do more this year), give talks on CyberSec concepts and exploits, and goof around a bit because that's half the fun when it comes to security. Also we watch Youtube Shorts of people parkouring in Minecraft. Don't ask why.
Can I sponsor you?
SCDT doesn't have sponsors *yet* since we're nowhere near as big as NJIT's NICC (they have 200+ members). But, we're always open for networking opportunities and things like this.
